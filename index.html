The Business Model

Creativity and Value Proposition

Danpharmaceuticals is a one stop e-shop to cater all your medical needs. Danpharmaceuticals operates on a brick and mortar model, that means that Danpharmaceuticals has a physical presence as traditional stores, as well as conducts business over the internet at any time of the day or night. This differentiates Danpharmaceuticals from its competitors and enables the company to provide its customers all the medical products that are needed via traditional as well as an e-medium. The customers prefer Danpharmaceuticals as they have the liberty to either head up to the traditional store or visit the online shop and order medicines online that guarantees same day delivery. Operating using the bricks and mortar model also enables Danpharmaceuticals to keep their prices low as compared to their competitors, as having a limited number of physical stores and encouraging their customers to order online allows them to minimize their costs and offer best prices.

Revenue Model

Danpharmaceuticals can be seen as a supply chain company that allows FDA approved drug manufacturers to sell their products at Danpharmaceuticals. Danpharmaceuticals generates revenue by indulging in B2B practices with the drug manufacturing companies and B2C practices with its customers. The company generates profit majorly by charging a commission on the drugs it sells and by other means, that include allowing relevant ads to be posted on the website “https//www.danpharmaceuticals.com”. The company believes that this revenue model that they have adopted has been successful for them and will prove to be even more successful in the future as the company is increasingly developing a name for itself and obtaining an increased market share in the pharmaceutical retail industry, thus becoming a need for the drug manufacturers to sell their drugs. In addition to this, Danpharmaceuticals is also looking to increase their revenue generation by tapping new doors such as allowing relevant companies to display their ads on the website, collaborating with existing transportation companies such as uber and doordash to make deliveries more efficient, etc.

Market Opportunities and Competitive Advatanges

What was once used to be “Western Medicine” is now produced and consumed globally. This eliminates all (if any) limits to the market size or segments the company intends to serve to. The company as of now offers its services in the US but also intends to extend its operations overseas. The most significant advantage that this firm brings to the market is the successful adoption of bricks and mortar model. Let’s have a look at how this has benefited the company in the current times. In the current times as the pandemic has slowed down almost every industry on the planet, including the pharmaceutical industry, Danpharmaceuticals has continued to thrive as the customers who while observing isolation protocols find it difficult to visit pharmacies to buy medicine can safely stay and order medicines from their homes.

Market Strategy

Danpharmaceuticals believe that as the world has witnessed a rapid ever-increasing use of technology, the “traditional media” to some extent is and soon would not be of much effect as a means of marketing. Thus, the company actively participates in marketing via digital media, for example running advertisement campaigns on social media platforms including facebook, instragram and youtube. That been said, Danpharmaceuticals also acknowledges the fact that a significant percentage of the target audience can be better reached out by marketing using the traditional media. So, Danpharmaceuticals also occasionally runs advertisement campaigns using traditional media as well, including local newspapers, radio shows and the bill boards.
